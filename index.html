About this Website

This website is a collection of information about the hit TV series on AMC "Breaking Bad". This website was created by our design team, the Breaking Bad group (Tiffany Tse, Jenny (Zhong) Yang, and Vivan D. Wang). The idea behind this website is to aimed a large demographic of people who either heard about the show, have watched some of the episodes, and finished the series.

We are trying to organize the information on quick summaries on each episode and give character biography and the actor's real bio. Overall we are trying to make people welcome to check out everything this site has to offer. Check out our collected information created by other Breaking Bad fans in a single website!
